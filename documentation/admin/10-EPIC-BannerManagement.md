**Priority**: P2 (Medium - Marketing)  
**Estimated Stories**: 15  
**Estimated Effort**: 4-5 days  
**Command**: `/admin-10-banners`

---

## Epic Overview

### Goal
Complete banner campaign management system for homepage and marketing with scheduling and A/B testing.

### Business Value
- Promotional campaigns management
- Time-based scheduling
- A/B testing capabilities
- Click tracking and analytics
- Conversion measurement

### Key User Stories (15 Total)

1. **BAN-001**: Banner List Page with Stats
2. **BAN-002**: Banner Editor Form (Title, Subtitle, CTA, Image)
3. **BAN-003**: Banner Placement Configuration (Homepage, Pages, Position)
4. **BAN-004**: Banner Scheduling (Start/end dates, time)
5. **BAN-005**: Banner Targeting (Device, Audience)
6. **BAN-006**: Banner Preview/Mockup
7. **BAN-007**: Banner Status Management (Active, Paused, Scheduled)
8. **BAN-008**: Banner Analytics (Impressions, Clicks, CTR)
9. **BAN-009**: A/B Testing Setup
10. **BAN-010**: Banner Priority/Order
11. **BAN-011**: Banner Duplication
12. **BAN-012**: Banner Archive
13. **BAN-013**: Banner Templates
14. **BAN-014**: Display Rules Engine
15. **BAN-015**: Banner Performance Reports
