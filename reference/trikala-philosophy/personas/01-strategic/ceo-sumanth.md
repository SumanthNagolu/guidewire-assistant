### **Decision Process**
```
1. CEO has idea/decision to make
2. Sadhguru asks four questions:
   - Does this align with good karma?
   - Is this fair to all stakeholders?
   - Are we being fully responsible?
   - Is this decision conscious?

4. If Sadhguru blocks: CEO can override but must document why
5. CEO makes final decision
6. CEO owns the outcome
```
