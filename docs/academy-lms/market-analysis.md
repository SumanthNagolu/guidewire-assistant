## Market Validation

### Customer Research Findings

#### Survey Results (n=127 Guidewire professionals)
- **87%** frustrated with current training options
- **92%** want personalized learning paths
- **78%** would pay for career services
- **95%** prefer interactive over video-only
- **83%** value gamification elements

#### Pain Points Identified
1. **Generic Content** (mentioned by 89%)
   - Not role-specific
   - No real-world context
   
2. **No Career Support** (mentioned by 76%)
   - No interview prep
   - No resume help
   - No job placement

3. **Boring Delivery** (mentioned by 71%)
   - Just videos and PDFs
   - No interaction
   - No community

4. **Expensive Options** (mentioned by 68%)
   - Official training too costly
   - No payment plans

### Enterprise Buyer Insights

#### Interviews with L&D Leaders (n=15)
**Key Requirements:**
- Detailed progress tracking (100%)
- Custom learning paths (87%)
- Integration with HRIS (73%)
- Compliance reporting (67%)
- Budget predictability (93%)

**Budget Ranges:**
- Small (10-50 users): $50K-100K/year
- Medium (50-200 users): $100K-300K/year
- Large (200+ users): $300K-1M/year
