# HR Activities - Complete Brainstorming
**Date:** December 2024
**Status:** Phase 1 Complete

## Overview

This document comprehensively maps all activities an HR professional performs across different timeframes, from daily operations to annual strategic initiatives.

---

## DAILY ACTIVITIES

### 1. Attendance & Time Management
- Review daily attendance reports
- Monitor clock-in/clock-out times
- Flag late arrivals and early departures
- Approve/reject timesheet corrections
- Track real-time attendance dashboard
- Handle attendance discrepancy queries
- Update attendance records for remote workers

### 2. Leave Management
- Review pending leave requests
- Approve/reject leave applications
- Check leave balance availability
- Process emergency leave requests
- Update leave calendars
- Notify managers of team absences
- Handle leave extension requests

### 3. Employee Queries & Support
- Respond to employee inquiries (email/chat)
- Resolve payroll questions
- Address benefits queries
- Handle document requests
- Assist with system access issues
- Provide policy clarifications
- Escalate complex cases

### 4. Expense Processing
- Review daily expense submissions
- Verify receipt uploads
- Approve/reject expense claims
- Flag policy violations
- Process reimbursement requests
- Update expense tracking
- Handle missing receipt cases

### 5. Documentation & Records
- Update employee information changes
- File and organize HR documents
- Scan and upload physical documents
- Maintain digital employee files
- Archive completed forms
- Update emergency contact information
- Record employee communications

### 6. Communication
- Send daily announcements
- Distribute policy updates
- Share company news
- Post job openings internally
- Communicate deadline reminders
- Send birthday/anniversary wishes
- Notify teams of schedule changes

### 7. Compliance & Reporting
- Complete daily compliance checks
- Update headcount reports
- Monitor labor law compliance
- Track mandatory break times
- Log safety incidents
- Report workplace accidents
- Maintain audit trails
