# InTime Bench Sales Organization & Process Blueprint

## I. Vision Alignment
Bench Sales is not a support function—it is the **revenue engine** of InTime. From Day 1, bench operations are designed as a streamlined system with clearly defined roles, precise handoffs, and full accountability at every stage.

**Mission:** Maximize consultant placements through precision, speed, and quality while strengthening InTime’s brand presence through professional digital engagement.

---

## II. Organizational Structure (Day 1 → Scalable Model)

### 1. Leadership Layer
**Senior Bench Sales Manager**  
- Owns **all consultants** and is fully accountable for placements.  
- Leads team meetings, sets daily targets, and monitors performance quality.  
- Manages **vendor/client relationships**, negotiations, and closures.  
- Generates new BDM opportunities through daily activities.  
- Reports directly to the CEO.  
- Oversees **delivery readiness**, ensuring BGVs, documentation, and compliance completion.  
- Builds and maintains **reference pipelines** from Day 1 to ensure future placement opportunities.  
- Receives candidates from the Talent Acquisition team, evaluates them, and submits detailed reports that include pros/cons, go/no-go status, estimated placement time, and a roadmap for placement.  
- Finalizes qualified profiles and hands them to Bench Sales Managers for marketing and submission.  
- Ensures all social and consultant profiles align with InTime’s brand and visibility standards.

### 2. Senior Execution Layer
**Bench Sales Manager**  
- Owns up to 10 consultants and manages full consultant life cycles.  
- Formats resumes, maintains hotlists, manages vendor communications, and oversees submissions.  
- Mentors recruiters to maintain submission quality and consistency.  
- Handles **delivery hand-in and hand-out** processes: receives profiles from the Senior Manager with consent, adds evaluation reports, verifies documentation and readiness.  
- Maintains audit trails, readiness summaries, and approval documentation.  
- Reviews and enhances consultant visibility and digital engagement on professional platforms.  
- Ensures compliance, client-readiness, and consistency in branding across all channels.

### 3. Portal Execution Layer
**Senior Bench Sales Recruiter**  
- Updates all consultant profiles across portals (Dice, Monster, Indeed) and maintains strong social visibility (LinkedIn, etc.).  
- Searches, collects, and uploads requirements to the Monday.com job board.  
- Executes submissions accurately and tracks outcomes.  
- Maintains complete and accurate Monday.com boards, including job links and updates from Managers.  
- Logs all submissions (email or network-based), ensuring consistent communication records.  
- Verifies data accuracy, updates candidate notes, and ensures social visibility aligns with job board activity.  
- Supports process documentation, compliance verification, and operational readiness.

## III. Process Flow (Assembly-Line Model)
> **Note:** Social engagement via LinkedIn posts, professional networking, and recruiter-candidate interactions are vital KPIs. Each member’s engagement activities (posts, comments, responses, and network growth) are tracked daily to measure visibility and branding impact.

### Step 1: Daily Kickoff (8:00–8:30 AM)
- Team call with CEO & Managers (all participate).  
- Review previous day’s metrics and outcomes.  
- Announce goals and individual priorities.  
- Identify high-priority requirements.  
- Record clear **targets and priorities** on the Monday.com “Daily Targets” board.  
- Log dependencies and blockers for transparency.  
- Track roadblocks, escalations, and achievements daily for review.

### Step 2: Portal & Data Updates (8:30–9:30 AM)
- **Recruiters:** Update all consultant profiles and enhance digital visibility.  
- **Managers:** Confirm consultant availability, rates, and review recruiter performance and engagement metrics.  
- **Senior Manager:** Validate readiness, ensure documentation compliance, and confirm brand alignment.

### Step 3: Requirement Sourcing (9:30–10:30 AM)
- **Recruiters:** Source jobs from key U.S. and Canadian job boards including Dice, Monster, CareerBuilder, LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, Workopolis, Eluta, Job Bank Canada, Glassdoor, TechFetch, SimplyHired, and Jooble. *Dexian and Collabera are handled by account teams.*  
- **Managers:** Match consultants, confirm job relevance, and ensure sourcing accuracy.  
- **Senior Manager:** Oversee quality assurance, remove duplicates, and approve relevant roles.

### Step 4: Submission & Marketing (10:30 AM–2:00 PM)
- **Managers:** Format resumes, handle vendor communication, and manage RTRs.  
- **Recruiters:** Execute submissions and ensure branding and communication consistency.  
- **Senior Manager:** Manage key vendor relationships and business development opportunities.

### Step 5: Response Handling (2:00–4:00 PM)
- **Recruiters:** Track responses, update statuses, and maintain clear communication.  
- **Managers:** Follow up on pending submissions and schedule interviews.  
- **Senior Manager:** Evaluate communication quality and manage escalations.

### Step 6: Reporting & Review (4:00–5:00 PM)
- All members present updates to the Senior Manager.  
- Senior Manager consolidates results and presents them to the CEO.  
- Reports include submissions, interviews, placements, and social engagement metrics.

## VIII. Quality Control Framework
### Weekly Social & Visibility Checkpoint
- Recruiter profiles reviewed weekly for consistency, compliance, and brand accuracy.  
- Consultant visibility and engagement levels validated and documented.  
- Results discussed during weekly meetings for actionable improvements.

### Gate 1: Consultant Verification
- Confirm availability daily, validate rates, and verify visa/location/skills.

### Gate 2: Resume & Submission Quality
- Standardize resume format; ensure >80% match; verify RTR completion.

### Gate 3: Follow-Up Quality
- Log vendor responses within 2 hours; track all submissions; document interview debriefs.

## XI. Implementation Checklist (Chronological Order)
### Pre-Day Preparation
- Verify Monday.com setup and member access.  
- Review portal logins and credentials.  
- Prepare documentation (BGV, references, compliance).  
- Confirm communication channels.

### Onboarding Day Actions
- Conduct team orientation.  
- Perform submission/reporting workflow dry run.  
- Optimize consultant profiles and visibility.  
- Set personal goals and activity templates.  
- Establish reporting cadence and escalation path.

### Post-Day Follow-Up
- Publish transparency reports for daily targets.  
- Collect feedback and identify improvement areas.  
- Schedule weekly reviews for consistency.
