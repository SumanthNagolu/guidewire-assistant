# InTime Training Organization & Process Blueprint (Talent-Led Model)

## I. Vision Alignment

Training is the **supply creation engine** of InTime — the foundation of the company’s transformation cycle. Every graduate from the Training Pod becomes a pipeline-ready consultant for the Bench Sales and Recruiting teams, ensuring a steady, qualified talent supply.

**Mission:** Deliver an 8-week transformation journey that converts trainees into client-ready consultants through structured onboarding, hands-on learning, and measurable performance outcomes.

**Strategic Goals:**
- Build job-ready talent with technical expertise, professional discipline, and delivery readiness.
- Maintain seamless collaboration with Talent Acquisition for sign-ups and with Bench/Recruiting Pods for placements.
- Ensure every trainee’s journey — from demo to certification — is fully trackable, measurable, and aligned with revenue goals.

---

## II. Organizational Structure (Day 1 → Scalable Model)

### 1. Leadership Layer — **Senior Training Manager (STM)**
- Acts as the **Program Director and Delivery Head** of the Training Pod.
- Owns all active trainees end-to-end; accountable for completion, certification, and bench readiness.
- Designs and updates **curricula** and **training programs** based on client needs and emerging technologies.
- Oversees demo sessions, orientations, and program launches; ensures consistency across trainers.
- Manages trainer recruitment, onboarding, and performance evaluations.
- Leads daily stand-ups and weekly retrospectives with coordinators and trainers.
- Reviews evaluation reports, approves certifications, and ensures readiness for bench or placement.
- Tracks overall KPIs: enrollment-to-certification rate, attendance, satisfaction, and placement conversion.
- Handles onboarding from Talent Acquisition sign-ups to active participation.

### 2. Senior Execution Layer — **Training Coordinator / Relationship Manager**
- Serves as the **primary liaison** between trainees, trainers, and management.
- Tracks attendance, progress, and milestone completion daily in Monday.com.
- Coordinates trainer calls, project timelines, and evaluations.
- Provides 24×7 trainee support and manages communication channels.
- Collects feedback and compiles weekly progress reports for STM.
- Conducts preliminary assessments and ensures data integrity for evaluations.
- Suggests program improvements based on recurring feedback or performance trends.

### 3. Execution Layer — **Trainers / Subject Matter Experts (SMEs)**
- Deliver training sessions, assignments, and evaluations according to approved curriculum.
- Maintain and update course materials to reflect InTime’s standards and market trends.
- Grade assignments, conduct mock interviews, and mentor underperforming trainees.
- Report performance data and session summaries to Coordinators and STM.
- Collaborate on curriculum updates, demos, and project supervision.
- Participate in trainer performance evaluations and feedback reviews.

## III. Process Flow — The Continuous Learning Model

> **Objective:** Maintain a rolling, measurable process from sign-up to certification with zero idle time and complete visibility.

### Step 1: **Talent Acquisition → Intake & Onboarding** (Day 0–1)
- TA forwards new sign-ups to STM with verified details.
- STM confirms enrollment, assigns a Coordinator and Trainer, and schedules orientation.
- Coordinator sets up LMS, Slack/Teams channels, and Monday.com access.

### Step 2: **Orientation & Demo Sessions** (Day 1–2)
- STM conducts a group orientation outlining program goals, timelines, and expectations.
- Trainers introduce the curriculum, methodology, and assessment criteria.
- Coordinators confirm attendance and resolve technical or onboarding queries.

### Step 3: **Active Training & Engagement** (Weeks 1–6)
- Trainers deliver daily sessions and manage assignments or projects.
- Coordinators monitor attendance, milestones, and engagement metrics.
- STM audits sessions weekly and reviews interim results for alignment.
- Weekly retrospectives document lessons learned and improvement actions.

### Step 4: **Progress Review & Intervention** (Weeks 6–7)
- Coordinators identify trainees at risk using dashboards.
- Trainers provide targeted mentoring or remedial sessions.
- STM approves intervention plans and reallocates resources where needed.

### Step 5: **Final Evaluation & Certification** (Week 8)
- Coordinators and Trainers conduct final evaluations and compile results.
- STM reviews outcomes, issues certifications, and hands off graduates to Bench/Recruiting.
- HR and Bench teams onboard certified trainees into active placement pipelines.

### Step 6: **Feedback & Continuous Improvement** (Post-Certification)
- Coordinators collect satisfaction surveys and performance feedback.
- STM analyzes data, updates curriculum, and reviews trainer performance.
- Continuous improvement actions are logged in Monday.com.

## VI. Monday.com Boards & Ecosystem

1. **Training Intake Board:** Trainee Name, Sign-Up Date, Coordinator, Trainer, Start Date, Orientation Date, Status.
2. **Training Students Board:** Attendance %, Modules Completed, Progress Notes, Evaluation Dates.
3. **Training Progress Board:** Daily updates, blockers, interventions, milestones.
4. **Training Evaluations Board:** Results, feedback, certification approval.
5. **Continuous Improvement Board:** Feedback patterns, curriculum updates, SOP actions.

**Automation Highlights:**
- New sign-up → Auto-notify STM & Coordinator.
- Missed attendance (2 days) → Escalation to STM.
- Certification approval → Auto-handoff to Bench & HR.

## VII. Quality Control & Trainer Evaluation Framework

**Weekly Quality Audits:**
- Review session samples for content accuracy and delivery quality.
- Validate evaluation consistency across trainers.
- Compare feedback with trainer performance metrics.
- Confirm all documentation and boards are updated and compliant.

**Trainer Performance KPIs:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|---------|
| Session Attendance | ≥95% |
| Student Satisfaction | ≥90% |
| Assignment Grading SLA | <24 hrs |
| Curriculum Adherence | 100% |
| Feedback Incorporation | 100% |

**Quality Gates:**
1. **Intake Validation** – Confirm trainee onboarding readiness.
2. **Mid-Program Audit** – Verify milestone completion and instructional quality.
3. **Certification Audit** – Review final evaluations before sign-off.
