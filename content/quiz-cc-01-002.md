---

### Question 1

**Question Text:**
What does Claim Maintenance involve in ClaimCenter?

**Options:**
A) Creating new policies
B) Handling claim updates, reserves, and payments
C) Setting up billing schedules
D) Designing workflows

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**
Claim Maintenance ensures the claim remains updated with correct reserves, payments, and recovery details.

---

### Question 2

**Question Text:**
What is a reserve in ClaimCenter?

**Options:**
A) Funds allocated for potential future claim payments
B) The total premium charged
C) An activity type
D) A workflow task

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
A reserve is money set aside to cover expected payments during claim settlement.

---

### Question 3

**Question Text:**
How can an adjuster correct an incorrect payment entry in ClaimCenter?

**Options:**
A) Modify the database manually
B) Void or reverse the payment transaction
C) Reopen the claim
D) Delete the payment record

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**
Incorrect payments can be voided or reversed, preserving a complete audit trail.

---

### Question 4

**Question Text:**
Which component manages financial calculations for payments and recoveries?

**Options:**
A) Financials Module
B) Assignment Engine
C) Rule Engine
D) Batch Processor

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
The Financials Module in ClaimCenter manages all payment, reserve, and recovery calculations.

---

### Question 5

**Question Text:**
Which ClaimCenter screen allows users to adjust exposures and view related transactions?

**Options:**
A) Team tab
B) Exposure tab
C) Summary tab
D) Search tab

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**
The Exposure tab lists exposures and related financial transactions for each claim.
