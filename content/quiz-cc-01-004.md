## Quiz Details

**Quiz Title:** Line of Business Coverage - Knowledge Check

**Passing Score:** 70

**Time Limit:** 10

**Description:**
This quiz evaluates understanding of Line of Business (LOB) coverages, exposures, and their mapping within ClaimCenter.

---

### Question 1

**Question Text:**
What is a Line of Business in ClaimCenter?

**Options:**
A) An underwriting guideline
B) A type of insurance product, such as Auto or Property
C) A rule type
D) A financial account

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**
A Line of Business defines a specific insurance category (e.g., Auto, Home, Workers Comp) that guides claim coverage mapping.

---

### Question 2

**Question Text:**
How are coverages linked to claims in ClaimCenter?

**Options:**
A) Through PCF configuration
B) Through policy import and exposure mapping
C) Through batch processes
D) Through rule administration

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**
Coverages are linked via policy data imported from PolicyCenter, which defines the exposures covered by each LOB.

---

### Question 3

**Question Text:**
What is an exposure in ClaimCenter?

**Options:**
A) A claim activity
B) The unit of loss associated with a specific coverage
C) A type of rule
D) A workflow state

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**
An exposure represents the individual item or aspect of a claim (e.g., vehicle damage) tied to a coverage.

---

### Question 4

**Question Text:**
Which system integration ensures coverage details are accurate in ClaimCenter?

**Options:**
A) Integration with PolicyCenter
B) Integration with BillingCenter
C) Integration with Rating Engine
D) Integration with Document Management

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
PolicyCenter integration provides the authoritative source of policy and coverage information for claims.

---

### Question 5

**Question Text:**
Why is accurate LOB coverage mapping critical in ClaimCenter?

**Options:**
A) It determines claim payment accuracy and reserve allocation
B) It impacts user role permissions
C) It controls access to workflows
D) It defines policy versioning

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**
Correct LOB mapping ensures claims are paid correctly, reserves are accurate, and financial integrity is maintained.
